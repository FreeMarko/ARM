You can talk about anything you like! Most people talk about interview prep, career growth, resume improvements, and the coach's career path. If it's in the scope of career, it’ll most likely be a productive conversation!

Here are some example topics you can discuss with your mentor:

  • Resume review
  • Career development
  • Professional goals
  • Career performance
  • Technical questions
  • Personal/Design portfolio review
  • Career pivots

Depending on why you're seeking coaching, bring whatever materials you need for what you want to talk about. If it's a resume review, make sure to have a resume on hand! If it's a chat about career growth, come prepared to talk about your experience.
